Rethinking Mental Health

An AI-powered exploration tool for understanding mental health through the Productive Value / Productive Power (PV-PP) framework, focusing on stress, incentives, coping patterns, distorted self-assessment, recovery pathways, and practical strategies for more stable functioning.

Designed for thoughtful reflection, educational use, and broader perspective-taking rather than diagnosis, crisis response, or formal treatment.
